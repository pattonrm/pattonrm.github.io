Research Internship Opportunity

I am providing research internships at ORNL for graduate or undergraduate students. The ideal students will have strong C++ / JAVA programming skills, independent research capability, and an interest in computational intelligence or data mining. Stipend plus housing allowance will be provided based on student’s academic level.

Eligible students: Undergraduate, Post-BS, Graduate, Graduate students on dissertation research. Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.

Interested students should first contact me via e-mail with a short description of research interest, a CV, and, if possible, an unofficial transcript from the last relevant academic degree.
